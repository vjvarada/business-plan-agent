### Headline: **RapidTools: From Intent to Production-Ready Fixture in 2 Hours**
---

### THE CORE INNOVATION (Centre panel — headline + sub-headline)

> **The Problem in One Line:** Any production engineer knows *what* fixture they need. None of them can draw it in CAD.
>
> **The Unlock:** RapidTools encodes 10+ years of expert tooling knowledge into software — so the engineer describes the operation, and the platform generates a validated, 3D-printable tool. No CAD training required.

### IS THIS FIRST-OF-ITS-KIND? (Right sidebar box)

**Honest answer: Incremental + First in Market**

> One European company — **Trinckle (Berlin)** — pioneered automated fixture software in 2013.
> Their customers include Audi Sport, Ford, VW, and Airbus.
>
> We are **NOT the first globally.** Trinckle proved the concept works.
>
> We ARE:
> -  **First in India and Southeast Asia** — Trinckle has zero India presence
> -  **First to support casting tools** (vacuum casting, sand casting = ~40% of India tooling)
> -  **First to use LLM intent capture** — Trinckle uses parameter forms; we use natural language
> -  **First full-stack** — software + hardware + consumables + services in one platform
> -  **First printer-agnostic** — Trinckle locks into Stratasys ($50K+ printers); we support ₹5K+ FDM printers

**Validated by the market:** Stratasys (NASDAQ: SSYS) acquired Trinckle's technology in 2025, integrating it into GrabCAD Print Pro at $5,000/year. This validates the category. **RapidTools is priced at ₹20,000/year** — accessible to Indian SMBs Stratasys will never serve.
