## Q&A Quick Answers

**Q1 — "Just software?"**  
Phase 1: design tool inside factory. Phase 2 (Yr3+): routing layer — spec in, best machine in network, ship 48hr. Honda printer = first node. **Software entry. Network business.**

**Q2 — "Why not Autodesk/Siemens?"**  
They'd cannibalise ₹2-4L/seat CAD revenue. Innovator's dilemma. Same dynamic that created Trinckle. **18-24mo structural window.**

**Q3 — "Stratasys enters India?"**  
GrabCAD Pro: $5k/yr vs our ₹2L. Stratasys printers: $50k+ vs ₹1.5L Fracktal. India team: 18mo to replicate our 6 VARs. **They'll partner. Can't compete.**

**Q4 — "AI gets cheap, someone copies?"**  
AI = interface, not moat. Moat = CAD kernel, ASME Y14.5, Honda IATF 16949 co-cert. **18 months co-development. You cannot fake the Honda stamp.**

**Q5 — "Services TAM too large?"**  
India tooling services directly addressable: ₹1,700-2,500 crore. Year 3 services = ₹3 crore = <1% of that floor. **Market not the constraint. VAR channel is the lever.**

**Q6 — "Honda — when does money come in?"**  
Q2 2026. ₹8L ARR, 5 seats. Honda already spent ₹10L on hardware — **procurement doesn't buy printers for software they're not licensing.** IATF = 7-year lock-in.

**Q7 — "Two founders, can you deliver?"**  
Team of 8 by Month 3. 80% platform reusable. Each new tool: 100 eng-hours. Honda + XDM co-develop with us. **Not building alone.**

**Q8 — "No Seed, what happens?"**  
Grant alone — ₹60L ARR + Honda/TVS logos — bankable for SIDBI debt + angels. **Default-alive on revenue. Seed = acceleration, not survival.**

**Q9 — "Why Karnataka?"**  
Honda Kolar. TVS 3hr away. Peenya. 6 VARs in KA-TN corridor. 15% India industrial output. Organically Karnataka. **TN/MH/TS have their own grants. This is Karnataka's category to claim.**

**Q10 — "Exit story?"**  
3 paths: Stratasys/Autodesk/PTC acquisition (5-8x ARR), IPO, PE secondary Series C. **Not engineering an exit. Engineering the category. Exit follows category leadership.**
