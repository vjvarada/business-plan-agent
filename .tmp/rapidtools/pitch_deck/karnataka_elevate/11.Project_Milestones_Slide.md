### GRANT CONTEXT — HOW ELEVATE FUNDING WILL BE USED

Karnataka Elevate Grant: **Non-Biotech Startup 6 months + 6 months**

Applied Additive will use the grant for:

- Engineering salaries (expand team from 2 to 6)
- Cloud infrastructure (CAD kernel APIs, LLM API costs)
- Pilot customer onboarding and certification (IATF 16949)
- VAR channel activation expenses (training, sales collateral, demos)
- Karnataka market field operations (customer visits, events, workshops)
