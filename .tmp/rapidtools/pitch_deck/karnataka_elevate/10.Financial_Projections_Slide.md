### KEY ASSUMPTIONS (Top section — 2 bullet boxes)

```

  ASSUMPTION 1 — Customer Growth from Warm Network

  We acquire customers through CEO's 15-year VAR network
  (6 reseller partners, 5,500+ manufacturing companies).
  Y2 target: 50 customers (1% VAR conversion). Y3: 120 customers.
  Base licence: $2,400/year (₹2L/seat). Average 1–5 seats.



  ASSUMPTION 2 — 3 Revenue Streams at Different Margins

  Software subscriptions (80% gross margin) anchor the model.
  Job-work services (25% GM) grow with each software customer.
  Hardware referrals (24% GM) are opportunistic, not primary.
  Blended gross margin improves as software mix increases.

```

## DESIGNER NOTES

**Slide Layout:**

- Top: 2 assumption boxes (side by side, navy outline)
- Centre: Revenue table (5 rows 4 cols, EBITDA row highlighted)
- Right sidebar: Year-by-year narrative (3 short bullets)
- Bottom: Use of funds single line in grey

**Highlight:**

- Total Revenue row: Bold, dark navy
- EBITDA FY2028-29: Green (+₹55L)
- EBITDA Y1 and Y2: Orange/amber (negative, acceptable at seed stage)

**Colour cues:**

- Negative EBITDA = amber, not red (intentional investment)
- Gross margin % row = light blue fill (shows progression 52% 60%)
