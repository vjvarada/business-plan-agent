## Channel Strategy: Autodesk VAR Playbook

### Why Channel-Based GTM?

**The Autodesk/SolidWorks Precedent:**

Autodesk generated **$4.5B in manufacturing revenue** with only ~200 direct salespeople by leveraging **1,000+ VARs globally** [^1][^2]:

| **Metric**          | **Direct Sales Only**                | **Channel-Based (Autodesk Model)**           | **RapidTools Advantage**     |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| **Market Coverage** | 50-100 cities (direct team limits)   | **2,000+ cities globally** (VAR reach)       | Immediate local presence     |
| **CAC**             | $35K (high touch sales)              | **$8K-12K** (partner-driven) [^8]            | 3-4Ã— more efficient          |
| **Sales Cycle**     | 3-6 months (direct outreach)         | **1-2 months** (existing relationships) [^8] | Faster conversions           |
| **Local Support**   | Limited (timezone/language barriers) | **Native language, same-day on-site**        | Superior customer experience |
| **Scalability**     | Linear (hire 1 rep = 1 territory)    | **Exponential** (1 partner = 10 reps)        | 10Ã— faster growth            |

**Why This Works for RapidTools:**

1. **Existing CAD Channel Infrastructure**: 1,000+ Autodesk/SolidWorks VARs already sell to our target customers
2. **Non-Competing Product**: RapidTools is operations-focused (tooling), not design-focused (CAD) - VARs can sell both
3. **Higher Margins**: VARs earn 30-40% on RapidTools ($720-960/seat) vs. 20-25% on Autodesk ($1,000-1,250/seat) [^1][^2]
4. **Easier Sell**: $2,400/year RapidTools vs. $5,000 Fusion 360 = lower friction, faster close rates

### The Autodesk VAR Model Applied to RapidTools

**What is a VAR?**

A **Value-Added Reseller** is an independent company that:

1. **Sells** RapidTools licenses to end customers (earns 30-40% margin)
2. **Trains** customers on software usage (earns training fees)
3. **Supports** customers post-sale (earns support contracts)
4. **Delivers services** like Design-as-a-Service (earns 50-60% margin on services)

**Typical VAR Profile:**

- 5-50 employees
- Sells Autodesk, SolidWorks, or PTC products already
- Has 200-1,000 existing manufacturing customers
