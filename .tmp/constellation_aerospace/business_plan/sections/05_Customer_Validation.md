# Customer Validation

> **Last Updated:** 2026-07-10
> **Project:** Constellation Aerospace
> **Status:** Draft

## Executive Summary

Constellation Aerospace is **pre-revenue** with no active pilots or signed LOIs. This section therefore focuses on **market-validated demand signals** rather than direct customer traction. We document (1) validated customer pain points across target verticals, (2) buyer willingness-to-pay benchmarks from competitor revenue data, (3) a target customer profile for early adoption, and (4) a structured pilot acquisition plan for the first 12 months.

The strength of the opportunity is validated by **third-party evidence**: Planet Labs' $734.5M backlog demonstrates that EO customers sign multi-year commitments [[1]](#ref-1); defense/intelligence agencies have growing budgets for commercial geospatial data [[2]](#ref-2); and precision agriculture adoption is accelerating in both the US and India [[3]](#ref-3). The immediate priority is converting this market pull into Constellation's first 5–10 design partners.

## 1. Validated Pain Points by Vertical

| Pain Point | Evidence | Constellation Solution |

| **Data overload** — too much raw imagery, not enough actionable intelligence | Demand shift from "data" to "insights" [[4]](#ref-4) | Analytics platform with automated change detection |

### 1.4 Insurance

## 4. Pilot Acquisition Strategy (Y0–Y1)

### 4.1 Target: 5–10 Design Partners by End of Y1

| Phase | Timeline | Activity | Target Outcome |
|-------|----------|---------|----------------|
| **Discovery** | M1–M3 | Outreach to 30–50 potential customers across 4 verticals | 15–20 qualified conversations |
| **Demo** | M3–M6 | Platform demos using third-party imagery; showcase analytics | 8–12 demo completions |
| **Pilot** | M6–M9 | Free/subsidized pilot programs (3-month duration) | 5–8 active pilots |
| **Convert** | M9–M12 | Convert pilots to paid annual subscriptions | 5 paying customers ($80K+ ACV) |

### 4.2 Design Partner Value Proposition

| What Constellation Offers | What Design Partners Provide |
|---------------------------|------------------------------|
| Free/discounted platform access (3–6 months) | Feedback on product-market fit |
| Dedicated customer success support | Use case validation and case studies |
| Custom analytics development for their vertical | Allow logo use and testimonials |
| Priority access to own-satellite data (when available) | Reference customer for future sales |
| Influence on product roadmap | Early renewal at preferential rates |

### 4.3 Pilot Success Criteria

| Metric | Threshold | Measurement |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| User engagement | >3 logins/week by primary user | Platform analytics |
| Alert accuracy | >85% true positive rate | Customer feedback loop |
| Time-to-insight | <4 hours (Phase 1, 3rd-party data) | Pipeline monitoring |
| NPS | >40 (promoter territory) | Survey at pilot close |
| Conversion intent | "Would pay" at proposed ACV | Exit interview |

## 6. Key Insights

- **Market demand is validated by proxy**: Planet's $734.5M backlog, government budget growth, and ICEYE's insurance success all confirm strong buyer intent for EO analytics [[1]](#ref-1)[[6]](#ref-6)
- **Edge AI demand is real**: The $2.15B satellite computing market validates the technical approach, even before Constellation's own satellites are operational [[8]](#ref-8)
- **The traction gap is the primary risk**: Pre-revenue status means Constellation must demonstrate product-market fit within 12 months to support fundraising timelines
- **Design partner strategy is the bridge**: 5–10 free/subsidized pilots in Y0 create the reference customer base needed for paid contracts and Series A
- **Government sales cycles are long** (12–18 months): begin outreach in M1 to have contract vehicles in place by Y2 when own satellites come online

---

## 7. Investment Implications

1. **Market pull is proven** — not by Constellation's traction, but by $1B+ in competitor revenue and growing government budgets
2. **Pre-revenue risk is acknowledged** and mitigated through a structured pilot acquisition strategy
3. **Seed funding must prioritize** two things: (a) build the analytics platform, (b) acquire 5+ design partners
4. **Government credential-building starts immediately** — FCC licensing, ITAR, defense procurement registration create regulatory moat from Day 1
5. **The fastest path to revenue validation** is defense — where budgets are large, data is valued, and Constellation's edge AI latency is most differentiated
